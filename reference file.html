What we did this year

  • 01. Complete overhaul of the enrollment funnel, reducing friction in the multi-step form and increasing mobile conversion rates by 14%.
  • 02. Migration to a more efficient local search strategy, prioritizing school-specific Google Business Profiles which drove record-high local intent traffic.
  • 03. Launched the "Scholar Stories" video series on Meta, which maintained a 40% higher retention rate than previous brand content.

Challenges

  • A. CPM inflation on Meta during Q4 peak season required defensive bidding strategies that momentarily increased acquisition costs.
  • B. Highly competitive search environment for "High School" keywords in the Western Cape, leading to a focus on niche long-tail SEO.
  • C. Attribution gaps between online leads and physical school visits remain a hurdle for perfectly accurate ROI modeling.
